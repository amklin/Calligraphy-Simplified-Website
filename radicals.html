Canvas Instructions

To use the canvas, click on settings in the upper right corner of the screen.

Each of the buttons that pops up toggles a different setting.

refresh: Clears the canvas. You can also use the escape key to clear the canvas.

visibility: Turns the canvas on and off without erasing what is currently on the canvas.

line_weight: Use the slider below the icon to adjust the line width.

color_lens: Use the color picker below the icon to change the color.

The canvas default is off. To turn on the canvas for the first time use the refresh button.

Warning: Don't change the window size while writing. If you change the window size, use the refresh button to ensure that everything functions correctly.

Chinese Radicals


Radicals are the backbone of Chinese characters. They can help you get an idea for the meaning or pronunciation of the character. They assist you greatly when trying to learn new characters.

There are 214 radicals, but you don't need to know all of them. Here are just some of the most commonly used radicals.

草字头 (cǎo zì tóu)

艹

Meaning: grass

  • Examples:
  • 草 (cǎo): grass
  • 花 (huā): flower
  • 茶 (chá): tea
三点水 (sān diǎn shuǐ)

氵

Meaning: water

  • Examples:
  • 海 (hǎi): ocean
  • 河 (hé): stream
  • 流 (liú): to flow
人字旁 (rén zì páng)

亻

Meaning: person

  • Examples:
  • 他 (tā): he, him
  • 位 (wèi): position
  • 做 (zuò): to work
提手旁 (tí shǒu páng)

扌

Meaning: hand

  • Examples:
  • 推 (tuī): to push
  • 拉 (lā): to pull
  • 打 (dǎ): to hit
言字旁 (yán zì páng)

讠

Meaning: speech

  • Examples:
  • 说 (shuō): to speak
  • 语 (yǔ): language
  • 讨 (tǎo): to discuss
口字旁 (kǒu zì páng)

口

Meaning: mouth

  • Examples:
  • 喉咙 (hóulóng): throat
  • 呢 (nē): particle, for questions
  • 听 (tīng): listen
女字旁 (nǚ zì páng)

女

Meaning: woman

  • Examples:
  • 妈 (mā): mother
  • 好 (hǎo): good
  • 姐 (jiě): elder sister
竖心旁 (shù xīn páng)

忄

Meaning: heart

  • Examples:
  • 怪 (guài): strange
  • 忆 (yì): memory
  • 快 (kuài): fast
木字旁 (mù zì páng)

木

Meaning: wood

  • Examples:
  • 树 (shù): tree
  • 林 (lín): forest
  • 校 (xiào): school
绞丝旁 (jiǎo sī páng)

纟

Meaning: silk

  • Examples:
  • 红 (hóng): paper
  • 纸 (zhǐ): paper
  • 线 (xiàn): line, thread
金字旁 (jīn zì páng)

钅

Meaning: metal

  • Examples:
  • 针 (zhēn): needle
  • 钱 (qián): money
  • 钟 (zhōng): clock
日字旁 (rì zì páng)

日

Meaning: sun

  • Examples:
  • 时 (shí): time
  • 晚 (wǎn): night
  • 明 (míng): clear, bright
